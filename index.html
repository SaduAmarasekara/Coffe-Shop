Our History

The Central Union of Western Agricultural Cooperative Societies (UCCAO) was created on October 17, 1958 with the main objective of marketing Arabica coffee from its member cooperatives.

The Agricultural Cooperative Society of Planters of Noun The Agricultural Cooperative Society of Planters of Mifi, Haut-plateaux, Koung-Khi (CAPLAMI-Bafoussam) The Agricultural Cooperative Society of Planters of Haut-Nkam.

On the commercial level, the success of certain: dealing with large tonnages, exporting its own coffee throughout the world, it has sufficient size to negotiate effectively with buyers.
